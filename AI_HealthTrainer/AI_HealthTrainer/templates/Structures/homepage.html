ABOUT US

The "AI Health Trainer" project combines artificial intelligence (AI) with Python, the Django framework, and Mysql to help users run personalized wellness programs. With MediaPipe, the project is able to analyze the user's body movements, while Django is used for interactive user interfaces. User health data and goals are stored in Mysql. AI will analyze this data and design individualized exercise and calorie programs, resulting in a smart and effective approach to health.
